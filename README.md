# American College Insights

## Description

This project is an analysis on an extracted dataset about American Post-Secondary institutions, their tuitions, and salary outcomes for various majors. The aim with this analysis is to allow people to observe cleaned, coherent data and have a tangible idea on return on investment for different majors

## Value Add

This provides actionable insights into the return on investment on different degrees so that American students can make an informed choice on what to major in

## Data Analysis

The data anlyzed comes from the following csv files:

- Historical Tuition
- Tuition Income
- Salary Potential

Historical tuition is analyzed in order to demonstrate the differences in tuition costs at private institutions and public institutions. It is also analyzed to demonstrate has tuition has changed over time. This is important so that prospective students can take note of the differences in costs between the institutions and how tuition prices have changed over time in order to understand financial implications behind their choices.

Tuition income is analyzed in order to demonstrate how much students of different income brackets have to pay for post-secondary education to further allow students to make an informed choice, given their income bracket.

Salary potential is analyzed in order to demonstrate the differences in salary outcomes between students who majored in STEM and students who did not major in STEM early in their career and later on in their career.
